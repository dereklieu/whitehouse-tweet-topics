Happening Now

Below are 27,346 @WhiteHouse tweets from Obama's presidency. Grouped by common meaning, some trends are revealing. For example, while "health care" is a theme throughout, "Obamacare" appears in mid-2013, pointing to a shift in communications strategy to own the by-then common name for the Affordable Care Act.

Other groupings, like "POTUS" or "Happening Now:" are lexical, and reveal different habits of the White House social media team.

I used k-means clustering, an unsupervised machine-learning algorithm, to achieve the groupings, then assigned logical titles. As with anything of this sort, oddities are to be expected. Learn more about my methods.
